ABOUT

Hey! I'm a passionate Information Technology student who loves building things and breaking them — in the best way possible. By day, I craft clean, functional web experiences as a Full Stack Developer, working across both frontend and backend to bring ideas to life. I enjoy turning complex problems into elegant solutions, whether that's designing intuitive UIs or architecting robust APIs.

But I don't just build — I also hunt bugs.
